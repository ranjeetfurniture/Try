FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to common questions about timelines, locations and scope of work.

Do you work only in one city?

No. RS takes up projects across India, with a focus on metro and high-growth locations. Travel and stay are planned as per project.

What types of spaces do you work on?

Residential homes, apartments, villas, cafes, bars, offices and retail interiors.

Do you provide only furniture or full interiors?

Both. Depending on requirement, RS can execute only furniture or complete interior fit-out including POP, electrical and more.

How early should we contact you?

Ideally before civil work finishes, so furniture, POP and services can be planned together from drawings.

Can you work with our architect or designer?

Yes. RS can execute from your existing drawings and coordinate with your design team on site.

What budgets do you work with?

Budgets depend on size, city and finishes. Basic details shared through the quote form help in giving realistic estimates.
