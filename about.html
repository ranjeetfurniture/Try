About

From drawing to delivered site

Ranjeet Sharma

Founder • RS Furniture & Interior Fit-Out

With 20+ years in furniture and interior execution, Ranjeet has worked across apartments, villas, offices, cafes and bars throughout India.

Every project is developed through clear 3D plans or detailed working drawings. This ensures that what you see on paper is precisely what is executed on site.

RS coordinates carpenters, POP teams, electricians, polish teams and other contractors so you work with a single point of responsibility.



Approach

How RS handles each project

1. Understanding the space

Review existing layouts, measurements and client requirements for furniture, storage, POP and services.

2. Drawings & 3D plans

Prepare 3D visuals or working drawings so scope, materials and finishes are clear before execution starts.

3. On-site execution

Execute work on site with regular supervision, quality checks and coordination with other contractors.

4. Finishing & handover

Final fittings, polishing and cleaning before handover, with basic after-service support as agreed.
